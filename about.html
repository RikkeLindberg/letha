About Letha

We are a family business located in Oslo, Norway. Every shoe are made with passion and good craftsmanship.

The leather and material we use are organic, and we are very proud to be a sustainable company, contributing to the envirorment and at the same time doing what we love.

Our goal is to provide our customers with the highest quality shoes and products. We want everyone to be happy with what we make and feel the luxury of having handmade shoes.

Meet the people behind Letha

This is Tor Hansen, he is 75 y/o, second generation shoemaker and founder of Letha. Tor has made leather shoes his whole life, with a passion for quality and sustainable materials.

This is Jacob Hansen, 30 y/o, everything he knows about the shoemaker profession he has learned from his grandfather Tor. He takes over the business when his grandfather retires.
